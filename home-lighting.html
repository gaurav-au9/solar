SOLAR HOME LIGHTING PRODUCTS

A home lighting system developed by kSPL will take input from a PV module which will
charge the high capicty battery.
The output from the circuit will run LED light and DC fan of 3 watt and 5 watt respectively and will
also provide mobile charging facility. The operation of the LED light is in three modes i.e. OFF, Low intensity and High intensity.
